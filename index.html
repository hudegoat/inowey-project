WELCOME TO STANDARD CHARTERED BANK

Standard Chartered Bank is a major British multinational banking and financial services company headquartered in London. Unlike many UK banks, most of its business comes from emerging markets across Asia, Africa, and the Middle East. The bank has a long history dating back to the 19th century and today operates in over 60 countries with around 85,000 employees worldwide. It provides a wide range of services including personal and business banking, wealth management, trade finance, corporate and investment banking, and digital banking solutions. In recent years, Standard Chartered has focused heavily on innovation and sustainability — for example, it has launched services related to cryptocurrencies for institutional clients, which makes it one of the first major global banks to do so. In Kenya, Standard Chartered Bank has been operating since 1911, making it one of the oldest foreign-owned banks in the country. It is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange and remains a trusted name for retail, SME, and corporate banking. It’s well known for its strong digital banking platforms, good customer service, and focus on responsible banking practices such as supporting sustainable finance and community projects. Many customers appreciate its reliable online and mobile banking, and it continues to be recognized for pioneering new products in the Kenyan banking sector.
